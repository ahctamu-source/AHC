Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning

Harness AI/ML to advance healthcare, public health, and interdisciplinary innovation—while emphasizing responsible, ethical, and equitable applications.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning to advance healthcare, public health, and interdisciplinary innovation. The AI/ML Chapter within the Aggie Health Collaborative will equip students with the tools to analyze data, design intelligent systems, and responsibly apply technology to complex health and societal challenges.

About the Initiative

The AI/ML Chapter acts as a hub connecting medicine, public health, engineering, data science, and ethics under a shared vision. Students gain hands-on experience with real-world datasets and build tools for patient education, environmental monitoring, and health communication—while addressing privacy, bias, consent, ethics, and equity.

Plan of Action

  • 1. AI/ML Skills Workshops
    • Fundamentals of Python, R, TensorFlow, healthcare-related datasets.
    • 3–4 workshops/year; 25+ attendees each.
    • Partner with CS/engineering faculty and student developers.
  • 2. Cross-Sector Integration Projects
    • Compostify: predictive models for waste diversion & methane reduction.
    • HPO: text-mining tools for research trends and writing feedback.
    • Community engagement: AI-powered apps for health education.
    • At least 2 collaborations in year one.
  • 3. Hackathons & Prototyping Nights
    • 1 hackathon annually; 50+ participants; 3–5 prototypes.
    • Partner with tech clubs, startups, and sponsors.
  • 4. AI Ethics & Policy Forum
    • Debates/panels on privacy, consent, bias, sustainability.
    • 2 ethics events/year; combined attendance 75+.
    • Leverage IRB, law school, and ethics faculty.
  • 5. Broader Impact & Outreach
    • Dashboards/apps to share results with community partners.
    • 1 outreach product per semester.

Conclusion

The AI/ML Chapter serves as connective tissue across AHC sectors, amplifying impact and enabling scalable solutions— combining technical innovation with ethics, equity, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
